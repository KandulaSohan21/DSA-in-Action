# Real-Life Use Cases of Circular Queue

Circular Queues are commonly used when resources are reused continuously in a cyclic manner.

---

## 1. Call Center Customer Support

Incoming customer calls are assigned to available agents in a round-robin fashion.

After the last agent receives a call, the next call is assigned back to the first agent.

---

## 2. CPU Round Robin Scheduling

Operating systems allocate CPU time equally among processes.

Each process gets a fixed time slice before moving to the back of the queue.

---

## 3. Music Playlist Loop

When the last song finishes, playback starts again from the first song.

---

## 4. Traffic Signal Systems

Traffic lights cycle repeatedly through different directions.

---

## 5. Circular Buffers

Video streaming, audio playback, and network communication use circular buffers to efficiently reuse memory.

---

## 6. Multiplayer Game Turns

Players take turns in a fixed sequence, returning to the first player after the last one.

---

## Why Learn Circular Queue?

Circular Queues are widely used in operating systems, embedded systems, networking, and real-time applications because they efficiently manage fixed-size resources without wasting memory.
