# Real-Life Use Cases of Gnome Sort

Gnome Sort is useful for understanding how local corrections gradually produce a fully sorted arrangement.

---

## 1. Airport Boarding Queue

Passengers should board according to priority.

If two neighboring passengers are standing in the wrong order, staff can swap them and recheck nearby passengers.

---

## 2. Classroom Seating Adjustment

Students may need to be arranged according to roll numbers.

The teacher checks neighboring students and corrects misplaced positions.

---

## 3. Event Registration Lines

Participants stand according to registration IDs.

Misplaced participants are swapped until the queue is correctly arranged.

---

## 4. Package Sorting

Packages moving on a conveyor belt can be compared with nearby packages and repositioned if necessary.

---

## 5. Small Task Prioritization

Tasks can be arranged according to urgency by repeatedly correcting neighboring tasks.

---

## Why Learn Gnome Sort?

Gnome Sort demonstrates:

* Neighbor comparison
* Swapping
* Incremental correction
* In-place sorting

These concepts help in understanding more advanced sorting techniques.
