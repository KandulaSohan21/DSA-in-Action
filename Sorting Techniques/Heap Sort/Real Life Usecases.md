# Real-Life Use Cases of Heap Sort

Heap Sort is commonly used when the highest-priority item must be processed first.

---

## 1. Job Portal Candidate Ranking

Recruiters receive thousands of applicants.

Candidates are ranked based on assessment scores.

The highest-scoring candidates appear first.

---

## 2. Task Scheduling Systems

Operating systems prioritize important tasks.

Higher-priority processes execute before lower-priority ones.

---

## 3. Online Gaming Leaderboards

Players are ranked according to scores.

Top players appear at the top of the leaderboard.

---

## 4. Stock Market Analysis

Stocks can be ranked by growth percentage.

Investors view top-performing stocks first.

---

## 5. Hospital Emergency Systems

Patients are prioritized according to severity.

Critical cases receive immediate attention.

---

## 6. Search Engine Rankings

Web pages can be ranked according to relevance scores.

Higher-ranked pages appear first.

---

## Why Learn Heap Sort?

Heap Sort introduces:

* Binary Heaps
* Priority Queues
* Tree-based data structures
* Efficient ranking systems

These concepts are heavily used in real-world software engineering.
