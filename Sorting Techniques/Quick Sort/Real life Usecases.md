# Real-Life Use Cases of Quick Sort

Quick Sort is commonly used when large datasets need to be sorted efficiently.

---

## 1. E-Commerce Product Sorting

Online shopping platforms sort products based on:

* Price
* Rating
* Popularity
* Discounts

Example:

Sort products from lowest price to highest price.

---

## 2. Stock Market Analysis

Trading platforms sort stocks by:

* Price
* Market capitalization
* Daily gains

---

## 3. University Rankings

Educational portals sort students based on:

* CGPA
* Marks
* Rank

---

## 4. Employee Salary Analysis

Organizations sort employee records by salary for reporting and analytics.

---

## 5. Hotel Booking Platforms

Travel websites sort hotels by:

* Price
* Rating
* Distance from destination

---

## 6. Food Delivery Applications

Apps can sort restaurants based on:

* Delivery time
* Customer ratings
* Pricing

---

## Why Quick Sort Is Popular

Quick Sort provides excellent performance for large datasets and is widely used in software systems that require fast sorting operations.
