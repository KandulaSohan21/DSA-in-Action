# Real-Life Use Cases of Selection Sort

Selection Sort is useful when we repeatedly select the best, lowest, or highest item from a group and place it in its final position.

---

## 1. Campus Recruitment Ranking

A company receives assessment scores from candidates.

To prepare a merit list, candidates are arranged from highest score to lowest score.

The system repeatedly selects the highest scorer and places them in the next rank.

---

## 2. Scholarship Selection

Universities rank students based on CGPA.

The highest CGPA student is selected first, followed by the next highest.

---

## 3. Sports Tournament Ranking

Players can be ranked according to points earned.

The highest-scoring player receives Rank 1.

---

## 4. Employee Performance Evaluation

Organizations sort employees based on annual performance scores.

Top performers appear first in reports.

---

## 5. Movie Awards Nomination Ranking

Movies can be arranged based on critic ratings.

Highest-rated movies appear at the top.

---

## Why Learn Selection Sort?

Selection Sort introduces the concept of:

* Finding minimum/maximum elements.
* Positioning elements correctly.
* In-place sorting.

These ideas are used in more advanced sorting techniques.
